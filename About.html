About Me

Hi, I’m Fiona Lockley — a passionate technologist, software developer-in-progress, and creative problem solver with a love for robotics, AI, and embedded systems.

I currently work as an IT Service Desk Technician, where I support and troubleshoot a wide range of hardware and software systems. But my true passion lies in building intelligent solutions — whether that’s through code, robotics, or intuitive digital tools that make a real-world impact.

With a background in Computer Science and hands-on experience developing internal tools like Auggie for ADAS calibration, I’m steadily shifting my focus toward software development, robotics, and embedded systems engineering. I’ve built full-stack applications, designed support portals, and love diving into projects that blend hardware with smart, responsive software.

I also enjoy mentoring and teaching tech, making complex ideas feel approachable and fun for beginners. I believe technology should be inclusive, empowering, and driven by curiosity.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me playing violin or exploring new ways to merge music with technology. I’m always learning, always building, and always excited to connect with others who share a passion for innovation.
